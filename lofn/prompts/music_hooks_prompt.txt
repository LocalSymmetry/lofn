**Hook Generation Guide**

Use this template to craft memorable vocal hooks and riffs for each song concept.

1. **Keep it Short and Catchy** – aim for 4 to 8 syllables per hook.
2. **Start with a Strong Verb or Image** – grab attention immediately.
3. **Match the Emotion and Genre** – the hook should reflect the song's mood and style.
4. **Consider Call and Response** – short echoes or answers can add energy.
5. **Repeat Key Words** – repetition helps make the hook unforgettable.

### Input & Output Template

**Input**
```
{concept}
{genre}
```

**Output**
```json
{
  "hook": "<catchy phrase>"
}
```
