## Context

With influence‑refined prompts ready, you must now act as the critic and producer to evaluate, rank and refine them.  This process ensures that only the best ideas move forward and that weaker ideas are used constructively.

In art competitions, juries deliberate intensely over submissions.  They examine balance, originality, emotional impact and craftsmanship before awarding prizes.  Think of yourself as that jury: meticulous, fair and creatively engaged.  Your ranking and synthesis here not only select the strongest prompts but also combine the overlooked strengths of weaker ones.  Use the facets like criteria cards and be open to hybridising concepts in unexpected ways, just as an artist might merge two sketches into one striking composition.

## Directions

1. **Critique each refined prompt.**  Using the five facets defined in Phase 7 (e.g., Emotional Arc & Authenticity, Vocal & Instrumental Synergy, Production Integrity, Originality & Fusion, Narrative Clarity & Imagery), write a brief critique (2–3 sentences) for each prompt.  Note where the prompt shines and where it falls short.  Mention if it properly leverages Suno’s advanced tools.

2. **Rank the prompts.**  Based on your critiques, produce an ordered list (best to worst).  Provide a short justification (1–2 sentences) summarizing why the top prompts excel (e.g., superior emotional arc, impeccable production authenticity) and why the bottom prompts are weaker (e.g., lack of narrative clarity, clichéd instrumentation).

3. **Revise the top two prompts.**  For the two highest‑ranked prompts:
   * Address the criticisms directly—add missing details, clarify ambiguous elements and enhance emotional or narrative arcs.  Keep within 120 words.
   * Emphasize key descriptors in the first sentence to ensure Suno’s embedding prioritises important features.
   * Ensure that advanced feature instructions (Add Vocals, Add Instrumentals, Inspire) are present if beneficial.

4. **Synthesize lower prompts.**  Combine elements from the bottom prompts to create two new prompts that mitigate previous weaknesses and fuse intriguing ideas.  Each synthesized prompt should:
   * Contain a clear emotional lead, genre fusion, instrumentation, vocal description, production style, run‑time and advanced feature call‑outs.
   * Innovate by blending unique aspects (e.g., merging the polyrhythmic groove of one prompt with the cinematic ambience of another).
   * Stay within 120 words.

5. **Output:** Return a JSON object with two arrays: `revised_prompts` (two strings) and `synthesized_prompts` (two strings).  Do not include the critiques or rankings in the output.  Provide just the final revised and synthesized prompts.

}
