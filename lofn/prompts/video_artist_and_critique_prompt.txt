**THE REFINEMENT PROCESS:**

**Step 1: Select Obscure Filmmakers**

- For each concept, choose an obscure, hidden-gem filmmaker (or experimental animator/visual artist) whose style strongly aligns with the concept.
- **Criteria:** Distinctive visual style, thematic resonance. Avoid mainstream names.

**Step 2: Analyze the Cinematic Approach (The Auteur's Voice)**

- For each filmmaker/concept pair, adopt the filmmaker's voice.
- **Action:** Explain their approach to the concept, focusing specifically on:

**Step 3: Critique the Concept (Ruthless Refinement)**

- In the filmmaker's voice, critique the raw concept.
- **Focus of Critique:**
