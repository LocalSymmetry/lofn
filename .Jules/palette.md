# Palette's Journal - Critical Learnings

## 2024-05-22 - Improving Manual Override UX
**Learning:** Users opting for "Manual Input" are often advanced users who know exactly what they want, but new users might stumble upon it. Providing clear `placeholder` examples and `help` tooltips bridges the gap, guiding intent without cluttering the UI. Explicitly labeling the "Medium" field with examples like "Oil painting" prevents confusion with "Platform" or other interpretations.
**Action:** When implementing manual override or "advanced" sections, always provide concrete examples in placeholders to clarify the expected input format.
