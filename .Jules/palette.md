## 2025-12-19 - [Accessibility] High Contrast Primary Actions
**Learning:** Light blue buttons (#0EA5E9) on white background fail WCAG AA contrast (ratio ~2.8:1). Darker shades (Sky 700/800) are required for accessibility.
**Action:** Use Sky 700 (#0369A1) for primary actions and Sky 800 (#075985) for hover states.
