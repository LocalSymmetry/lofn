## 2025-02-24 - Streamlit Action Button Clarity
**Learning:** Streamlit's `st.button` `icon` and `help` parameters significantly improve scannability and accessibility without custom CSS. Using standard Material icons (e.g. `:material/lightbulb:`) ensures consistency.
**Action:** Always check `st.button` calls for missing `icon` and `help` parameters in Streamlit apps to provide quick UX wins.
