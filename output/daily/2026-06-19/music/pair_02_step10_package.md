## Facets & Guide

- **Sonic thesis:** an inventory of ordinary objects sung as a prayer to the quiet itself — the measurement ("twenty-four hours of nobody dying," carried as *"I counted twenty-four"* and *"a whole day of nobody. So far."*) becomes the chorus's reason to beg the morning to stay.
- **Triple-Arch hook image:** *a coat kept on by the door, a second cup set down for someone who may come* — instantly graspable, strange enough to hold the whole cathedral: relief that refuses to sit down.
- **Four-hook architecture (v5.5):** *Melodic* — the falling two-cell address "You. The quiet… / You. The morning…" (D minor reaching toward F, never landing). *Rhythmic* — the faint footwork stutter held under restraint, plus the counted "One. Two. Three." heartbeat. *Instrument* — the felt-piano dead-center note answered by a far-right high sine (the L→R call-and-answer). *Textural* — tape hiss bed + the **Sepia Inversion** reverb blooming on the downbeat; the kettle's single warm tick at the end.
- **Register discipline:** AWE→ache. No belt anywhere; close-mic mezzo lowered toward a hum; doubles bloom only a half-step wide and only at the prayer. The Solarpunk green is *dimmed*, not bright.
- **Why couplet chains:** AA BB CC = short held breaths; each couplet is one object tested and set down, so the form enacts a survivor metering out trust one small true thing at a time.
- **The un-naming (Persistence of Memory + the refusal to resolve):** the bridge explicitly *will not say the word* for the feeling — it substitutes objects ("the kettle. And the cup.") — which is how the obscure target emotion stays **felt and never named**, and how the ceasefire grief refuses to resolve.
- **Decay, not climax:** the song strips toward the end (doubles fall to one voice; harmony, then piano, then everything but drone and hiss) — the relief never fully lands.

## Production Notes
- **Vocal fingerprint:** close-mic female mezzo, A3–E5, dry chest-to-mix lowered toward a hum; breath-grain on every phrase end; **no belt, no melisma, no runs anywhere**; self-doubles bloom only a half-step wide and only on the prayer-chorus, then fall back to a single voice in the Final Chorus. **No reverb on the lead** except the deliberate Sepia Inversion send.
- **Signature device — the Sepia Inversion:** route a warm vintage plate reverb so its bloom is gated to the *downbeat* (present tense), not the decay tail (the wistful past). This is the Sepia-Toned Reverb *used against itself* — the flair turned present-tense per the meta-prompt. It must be audible by the first pre-chorus.
- **Held footwork stutter:** the juke/footwork pulse (S2) is present as a faint tick *under* the restraint and is **never released into a drop** — it is the body kept in the room (Summer/Jameson) without breaking the hush. If Suno tries to resolve it into a beat, pull it back via the Exclude field.
- **Spatial map:** felt-piano left-of-center; low D drone under the floor (mono); high luminous sine far right; the L→R answer rises through the pre-chorus (S3 bilateral call-and-answer). Vinyl crackle + tape hiss as a continuous bed.
- **Arrangement decay:** density subtracts across the back half — harmony drops, then piano, then everything but drone + hiss. The relief never fully resolves; the last sung line is the un-resolving "So far."
- **Body noises (declared):** intro breath + kettle click; mid-song "a long exhale, almost a word"; the counted heartbeat ("One. Two. Three."); the final single kettle tick. Distributed across the arc.
- **Hook recurrence:** prayer-hook in Chorus 1 + Chorus 2 + the **mutated/stripped Final Chorus** (meets the ≥2 + 1 mutation mandate).

## Major Deviations
- **No drop, by design.** Against the default expectation of a green synth-pop chorus, the song *strips* at the climax instead of blooming — required to keep "relief that doesn't trust itself" felt and unresolved. Justified by the Glass/Jameson gates and the seed's "refuse to resolve the ceasefire grief."
- **Measurement carried as fragments, not a literal chorus line.** The assignment's "let the measurement BE the chorus" is honored obliquely: the count ("I counted twenty-four… a whole day of nobody. So far.") seeds the chorus's plea rather than being shouted as a hook — a deliberate restraint-mode reading of the AWE→ache register.
- **"Trust" scrubbed from all sung lines.** Early drafts used "don't you trust it yet"; replaced with "don't you leave me yet" so the obscure emotion is never named even by its mechanism — only enacted (the coat, the un-naming bridge).
