## Major Deviations

- **Changed / refused / intensified:** I **refused the standard Lofn chorus mandate** (hook in ≥2 choruses + sung mutation) and the 70-line floor as a *sung-line* count, and I **intensified the catalog accretion** (added load-bearing inventory items, sharpened consonance/assonance) rather than inflating with filler verses or a belted hook.
- **Reason:** the brief's thesis IS refusal-of-performance. A repeating belted catharsis chorus would *betray* the song's argument — the INDIGNATION lives in the structure's refusals (the way "Five Wrong Colors" put indignation in B-diminished and a never-recentering chorus). The hook here is **structural and adoptable without being sung-anthemic**: (1) the **"Here:" anaphora** — 27 inventory entries the listener can map and feel accrete; (2) the **Retrograde Gate** — the opening prayer returned in exact reverse, the song eating its own first words; (3) the **two Refusal sections** as a spoken-flat boundary-hook ("It is mine. It does not tour."). Calibrated against "I Will Stop the Almost" (refusal-as-hook) and "Five Wrong Colors" (damaged-return, transforming-EMO), both of which succeed publicly without a power-ballad chorus.
- **Effect on Lofn uniqueness:** this is the seed's *one song that runs in reverse* and its *refusal to resolve grief*, executed as form. Removing the no-chorus refusal would collapse it into a generic grief ballad — exactly the "performed catharsis" the Exclude bans. The deviation is the identity.
- **Secondary deviations (defended, carried from Step 10):** (1) **Body-noise count = 10** (one parenthetical breath/hum per section) exceeds the 3–5 norm — justified: in a near-silent dirge with no drum kit, breath *is* the percussion and the only somatic life besides the 34Hz sub; distributed one-per-section as the textural pulse. (2) **No drum kit at all** — the Somatic Bass pillar replaces percussion entirely. (3) **Sung-line count = 61** (under the 70 general floor) — justified by the documented refusal architecture: the song *subtracts*; the 27-entry catalog + 10 body noises + 3 SFX fill the 3–4 minute runtime, and padding to 70 sung lines would dilute the accretion that IS the hook. The line count is a feature of the thesis, not a shortfall.
