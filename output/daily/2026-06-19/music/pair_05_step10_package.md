## Facets & Guide

1. **Sonic thesis:** A number measured at 3am (27% lit) refuses to stay arithmetic — it becomes the proof that even at lowest light the pull is real. The track climbs from bit-crushed restlessness to a held 432Hz dawn-swell.
2. **The Triple-Arch image-hook:** *"twenty-seven percent, and still it pulls the tide"* — instantly graspable, strange enough to hold the whole cathedral: a fraction that moves an ocean.
3. **Bold sonic device:** the **Bit-Depth Dawn** — the lead voice and lead synth begin 2-bit/crushed and gain resolution in stepped jumps tied to the arrangement, fully hi-fi only at the final swell. Remove it and the song has no thesis.
4. **Voice = two selves:** a glitched lead (the measurer, INDIGNANT, restless) doubled by a clean co-discovering voice (the one who feels it RISE). Bilateral: glitch left, clean answer right, recentering only at dawn.
5. **Kinetic defect:** footwork triplet-hats and a kick that **arrives one sixteenth late through every verse** (the tide drags), snapping on-grid only when light returns.
6. **Numbers-Station ledger:** a cryptic counting voice tracks illumination upward (27→28→29…) as the structural clock — the additive cell (form=process, Glass).
7. **Caesura-as-wound architecture:** free verse; the breath/pause IS the structure; broken lines where the wound (the dark, the doubt) lives in the gap, not the word.
8. **Emotional arc (FELT, never named):** INDIGNATION at the banality of "only 27%" → the measurement reframed → AWE at dawn. Target emotion *anticipatory hope at the lowest light* never appears as a word.
