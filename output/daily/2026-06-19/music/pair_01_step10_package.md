## Production Notes

- **Tuning/key:** 432Hz reference, A Mixolydian. The natural-7 → flat-7 colour keeps every cadence luminous but unresolved — a question held open across 3,500 years.
- **The choir-of-one:** take the lead vocal, granulate it into phoneme grains, and apply a *recognition automation*: Cell 1 is dry, cold, hard-panned ~70% LEFT with formant-shift detune (machine grain). Each subsequent cell warms — pitch correction relaxed toward her real contour, pan migrating toward centre-right, a touch of breath restored. By the Final Chorus the choir-of-one is fully human, in tune, hard RIGHT then blooming wide. This automation *is* the song's emotional arc; protect it in the mix.
- **Bilateral rise (S3):** the additive cell calls from the LEFT and is answered, pitched higher, from the RIGHT — and the answer's pitch floor climbs a little each chorus (horizon rising L→R, per "Starry Night II").
- **Drone (the tall tree):** a sustained low A centre, Mellotron-flute + soft sine, the vertical anchor under everything. Never let it drop except the bridge swap.
- **Kinetic defect (Summer gate):** restless triplet hi-hats under the floor with a limping, asymmetric swing that misses the downbeat — present in verses/choruses, gone in the bridge so the Measurement lands in earned stillness, then back for the Final Chorus.
- **Signature device timing:** Additive-Grammar Cell audible by 0:20; bridge stillness ~ the 2/3 mark; the human-contour turn lands on the Final Chorus and must be unmistakable in isolation.
- **Crackle bed (flair):** thin vinyl crackle + faint tape hiss under the whole track — the archive breathing — surfacing alone in the intro and outro.
- **Mix discipline:** lead +3dB, crystal-clear, no wash; reverb is a short plate on the choir-of-one answers only; sub stays light (this is AWE, not the somatic arm). No acoustic kit, no acoustic guitar — bio-ambient synthetic palette only.

## Major Deviations

- **Refused literal translation.** The seed's dangerous permission is honored to the letter: the Minoan phonemes stay **untranslated** and carry the hook. I deliberately did NOT render the prayer's meaning in English — translation would name what must stay felt, and would collapse anagnorisis into trivia.
- **Made the hook *mutate* rather than merely repeat.** The contract asks for hook recurrence + one transformation; I intensified this into the song's spine — the phoneme cell travels from affectless machine grain (left, cold) to human contour (right, warm, in time). The decisive blow is structural, not decorative.
- **Earned the stillness instead of staying still.** Jameson + Summer pull in different directions (embodied-now vs. move-a-body). I resolved the tension by keeping the limping triplet pressure alive through the verses and *removing* it only for the Measurement-bridge, so the number lands in silence the body had to stop moving to hear.
- **Anchored every accretive line to the room.** To pre-empt the sepia-nostalgia failure, each growing verse is pinned to a present-tense domestic object (kettle, chair-creak, fogged window, cup, the click-off). The accretion is the grammar assembling — but it never leaves the evening.
