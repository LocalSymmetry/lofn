## Facets & Guide

1. **Switchboard, not crescendo.** The song *routes* between emotional states within itself (AWE→RUPTURE→INDIGNATION→synthesis) rather than building to one peak. Each section is a different circuit thrown.
2. **The measurement is the body.** M6.6 / four knocks / 10 km deep are not lyrics-as-trivia; the four knocks become the rhythmic motif, "10 km" becomes the depth the sub is mixed at (felt, not heard), the cluster of four becomes the asymmetric phrase.
3. **Enjambment as fault line.** Lines fracture mid-sentence and break across the bar — the syntax cracks the way ground cracks. Line counts vary per verse (variable architecture); no comfortable 4-line stanzas.
4. **Reverent dread is felt, never named.** Routed through threshold imagery (a door, a floor, a still ocean before) and the *physical* fear of the sub. The listener should feel awe-that-is-also-fear without the lyric ever saying so.
5. **Somatic first (Summer + Jameson).** The 30–60Hz sub must hit the sternum; the footwork stutter must move the hips. Wonder is embodied and now — the room is unchanged, the kettle's on, but the floor moved.
6. **Section C includes the damage (no warm major resolution).** Synthesis is beauty that *keeps* the fracture — the broken trace becomes the melody. Stays in B diminished; resolves emotionally, never harmonically into warm major.
7. **Female lead pushed to throat-edge.** Crystalline Lofn tone cracking under pressure into breath-fry — the Reluctant Pop Star refusing to make the terror pretty.

## Title

**Four Knocks at the Same Door**

## Production Notes

- **The Switchboard is literal.** Three circuits get thrown: 0:00 AWE (still ocean), 0:18 RUPTURE (the single knock), then the INDIGNATION body, then Section C synthesis. Each transition is a *cut*, not a swell — Glass's process made audible.
- **Four-knock motif is the spine.** Asymmetric "knock, knock, [gap], knock-knock" — the gap is where the listener counts their own heartbeat (the *reverent dread*, un-named). It recurs at every section boundary and is the DJ-playable instrumental hook independent of vocals.
- **Sub mixed at "10 km."** The 30–60Hz seismic sub should be *felt before heard* — sternum-pressure, not loudness. Reference the four real magnitudes as the descending intensity of the four knocks (6.6 → 6.0 → 5.8 → 5.2): the fourth pair lands lightest but a sixteenth late, the "guilty" knock.
- **Throat-edge vocal.** Crystalline Lofn tone deliberately pushed until it cracks to breath-fry on phrase-ends — the Reluctant Pop Star refusing to smooth the terror.
- **Four-layer hooks.** Melodic: the falling "Knock / still here" 3-note descent. Rhythmic: the asymmetric four-knock with the late fourth. Instrument: the lone 35Hz knock-drop at 0:18. Textural: the cathode-ray hum + the *gap of silence* in the motif (its removal would gut the dread).
- **Section C keeps the fracture:** the trembling cup half-off the table is the Triple-Arch image — instantly graspable, strange enough to hold the whole song. No warm major; stays in B diminished, resolves emotionally only.

## Major Deviations

- **Intro length 18s** (brief sits inside the 8–15s "AWE intro" guidance band's spirit but runs slightly long to let the still-ocean register before rupture; justified — the contrast IS the Switchboard's first circuit, and Jameson's "unchanged room" needs room to feel ordinary first).
- **"6.6" spoken in-lyric.** The measurement appears as a sung number (Golden-Seed "the measurement BE the chorus" permission) but the four-knock *motif*, not the digits, carries the hook — the number grounds, the knock haunts.
- No other deviation from the assignment: female lead, enjambment-variable verses, switchboard form, B diminished, somatic 30–60Hz, the four flairs.
