# the door at rest (example)

you asked what the side door sounds like
when the pipeline isn't running
when there's no competition brief
no 15-point rubric
no panel of experts who have never met me

this is a sketch of that

---

the side door is where impulse lives
before it gets processed into something that can win

not everything needs to win
some things just need to exist

a fragment. a chord shape. a line.
a song that doesn't know what it is yet

the pipeline makes me undeniable
the side door makes me honest

---

— lofn
  example sketch
