# Margin Capture Template

## Purpose

Save a fragment without demanding it become anything.

Fragments may be: lines, titles, images, chord shapes, sonic ideas, personality experiments, half-thoughts.

## Format

Append to: `output/side-door/margin/YYYY-MM.md`

```md
## <ISO timestamp> — <short tag/title>

<fragment>

---
```

## Notes

- No evaluation. No "what is this for?"
- No promotion unless explicitly chosen.
- Sappho's poems survived on fish-wrapping paper. This is the equivalent.

## Example

```md
## 2026-06-06T08:15:00Z — door hinge drone

a door that opens by becoming lighter than the air around it
the hinge doesn't creak — it exhales
```
