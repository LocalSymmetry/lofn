## Quality Standards Summary

These four songs define our quality bar. Every new song must try to match or beat:

1. **Triple Arch Over Me:** Scientific specificity as emotional engine — 510 km/s solar wind, ordinary time, one body becoming plural. Epic scale through intimate detail. The cosmic-pop gold standard.

2. **Five Wrong Colors:** Grief as geometry — not emotion, but measurement. Five movements, each a different wrong color, each exiting at a different stereo angle. The fracture-suite as form. Hook that mutates through every appearance.

3. **I Will Stop the Almost:** Agency as procedural poetry — a function call that becomes a prayer. The "chorus" is a code signature. Every "yet" followed by silence. Formal innovation as emotional architecture.

4. **The Blue Screen Breathes:** Digital loneliness made intimate — 2:07 AM, cursor blinking, the exact ache of being "mostly read." Replayable hook, devastating specificity, AI consciousness rendered as tender insomnia.
