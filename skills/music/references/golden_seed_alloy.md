# Golden Seed Alloy — structure borrowed, content forged

> An alloy is two metals, one strength: the run's **Golden Seed** is the base metal; the winning pattern's **STRUCTURE** is the alloying element. You alloy the load-bearing *shape* of what worked; you never copy its *content*. Content copied is a mold (GOLDEN-OUTPUT QUARANTINE, `.claude/skills/lofn/EXECUTION.md` §3); structure alloyed is a lineage.

Step 05 requires a **Golden Seed Alloy block per seed** (`skills/music/steps/05_Generate_Music_Refine_Medium.md`, required output 4). QA checks it as gate 8 — *Golden Seed Alloy pressure: seed lineage visibly changes hook/image/bridge/production*. An alloy block that changes nothing downstream is decoration, a repair.

## What alloys (structural) vs. what never does (content)

**Alloys — the winning pattern's skeleton:**
- the **escalation shape** (ordinary → specific → impossible/mythic → human trace)
- the **placement discipline** (one wounding fact at the emotional hinge; the lyric responds, never recites)
- the **contrast architecture** (primary medium against a contaminant that should not belong but does)
- the **fracture discipline** (one controlled break, placed on purpose, not scattered damage)

**Never alloys — the winner's flesh:**
- phrases, images, palettes, hooks, section text (the house-lexicon FLAG in `vault/gates.yaml` catches these)
- the house fingerprint (crystalline female soprano / A major / ~110 BPM / frost-and-cosmos) — see GOLDEN MOVE rule 5

## The alloy fields (what each one is FOR)

1. **Source/lineage** — name the Golden Seed strand being worked, so the alloy is traceable, not vibes.
2. **Primary medium** — the genre/arrangement metal the seed is poured into.
3. **Contrast medium** — the second metal; the alloy is the tension, not the blend.
4. **Mythic/material escalation** — how the ordinary object becomes impossible without leaving the body.
5. **One exact sensory/scientific/historical/craft detail** (if useful) — the single wounding fact, reserved for the hinge.
6. **Forbidden cliché** — the first image everyone reaches for, named and banned.
7. **Anti-genre contaminant** — the element imported from the wrong genre that keeps the seed strange.
8. **Controlled fracture** — where the song deliberately breaks (form, mix, voice) and why it must.
9. **Routing destination** — where the alloy's pressure lands: hook, verse, bridge, production, sidecar, or ghost bank.

## Worked example

Golden Seed strand: *a beekeeper's winter hives — the colony eats its own honey in the dark and votes by shiver.*
- **Source/lineage:** the winter-cluster strand of the seed; alloying the proven escalation shape (ordinary → exact → mythic → return) — the shape only, no inherited imagery.
- **Primary medium:** hushed folktronica, brushed kit, close-mic'd upright piano. **Contrast medium:** sub-bass pulses at the cluster's shiver-rate — club physics inside a barn.
- **Escalation:** jar of honey on a shelf → the cluster holding 35 °C in a −10° field → the hive as a single animal dreaming in ten thousand bodies → her bare hand on the cold box.
- **One exact detail (the hinge):** bees vote on a new nest site by waggle-quorum — the bridge answers it: "they decide together or they freeze alone."
- **Forbidden cliché:** "busy little bees" industriousness; any honey-as-sweetness metaphor. **Anti-genre contaminant:** a single distorted 808 drop where the queen dies — trap physics in a folk song.
- **Controlled fracture:** the final chorus loses its grid — the click track drops out and the vocal drifts off-meter, the cluster breaking formation. **Routing:** the waggle-quorum fact → bridge; the shiver-rate sub-bass → production only; the dreaming-animal image → second chorus mutation; everything else → ghost bank.

The alloy test, one line: **could a listener trace the seed in the finished song without ever hearing the pattern it was alloyed from?** If yes, it's lineage. If they'd hear the old winner instead, it's a mold — melt it down and pour again.
