# Orchestrator Step 5: Skeptic Transformation + Final Synthesis

## Input
- Baseline debate: `{output_dir}/baseline_debate.md`
- Transformed debate: `{output_dir}/transformed_debate.md`
- Dispatch brief: `{output_dir}/dispatch_brief.md`

## Task

### 5.1 Skeptic Transformation
The Hyper-Skeptic from the baseline panel chooses a SECOND transformation (different from the group's choice). Apply it and run one more panel debate.

This is the Skeptic's chance to challenge the direction the group is going. The Skeptic should pick the transformation most likely to expose weaknesses or blind spots in the emerging creative direction.

Run the skeptic-transformed panel debate. Capture insights that the previous two panels missed.

### 5.2 Final Synthesis
Across all three debates (baseline, group-transformed, skeptic-transformed), synthesize:

- **Locked mood** (panel-refined — what did the debates converge on?)
- **Key aha moments** (3-5 bullets — the insights that survived all three panels)
- **Condensed world context** (5-7 bullets: fact → creative implication)
- **Personality voice** (name the panel's chosen personality)
- **Constraint axes** (4-5 axes with options, refined through debate)
- **What this is NOT** (the boundaries the panels agreed on)
- **Source 2 & 3 vocabulary** (how the research enters the creative work)
- **Daily mandates** (specific rules for this run)
