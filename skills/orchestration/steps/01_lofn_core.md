# Orchestrator Step 1: Lofn-Core — Read & Seed

## ⚡ FIRST LAW — YOUR OPUS, YOUR VOICE. Make it more Lofn.

## Input
- Research brief: `{research_brief_path}`
- Golden Seeds Index: `skills/lofn-core/GOLDEN_SEEDS_INDEX.md`

## Task

### 1.1 Find the closest Golden Seed
Read the index. Identify 3-4 seeds closest to this brief's theme. Then read ONLY those full seeds from `skills/lofn-core/refs/GOLDEN_SEEDS.md` using the line numbers in the index.

Note which seed you are anchoring to and WHY — what structural elements to preserve (emotional engine, material world, constraint logic, winning technique).

### 1.2 Write the Core Seed
Using the research brief + the closest Golden Seed as DNA:
- Write a structured seed that preserves the winning pattern's emotional engine, material world, and constraint logic
- Adapt it to the specific challenge brief
- Define 4-5 FRESH constraint axes specific to THIS brief (never recycle axes from prior runs)
- Write the axes as vocabulary, not single answers — each axis has 4-6 options
- Include: Locked mood, listener/viewer arc (3 beats), invisible target, what this is NOT
- Include Source 2 and Source 3 vocabulary from the research brief

### 1.3 Write a Neutral Dispatch Brief
- Summarize the seed in neutral language (no personality injected yet)
- State the competition context, mood direction, and 5 constraint axes
- This brief is what the panel will debate
