# Archetype: THE MEASUREMENT

Use when one number/fact becomes the emotional hinge.

## Emotional Territory
Re-enchantment through precision. Knowledge stops being trivia and becomes awe, dread, tenderness, or responsibility.

## Structural Template
1. Ordinary human-scale scene.
2. Mundane sensory details before the fact.
3. The measurement arrives as hook or pre-chorus turn.
4. Same scene is re-seen under the fact.
5. Final return: the number becomes prayer or vow.

## Risks
- Fact as trivia, not emotional voltage.
- Too many facts; only one should dominate.
- Explaining science instead of letting it reframe the body.

## Example
Eight Minutes: cooking dinner by sunlight; fact “eight minutes to get here”; final hook “Eight minutes, keep coming.”
