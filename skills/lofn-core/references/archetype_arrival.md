# Archetype: THE ARRIVAL

Use when the song/image begins with first encounter: the moment a place reorganizes scale.

## Emotional Territory
Awe through arrival. The singer/viewer steps into a place and becomes newly small, included, or reoriented.

## Structural Template
1. Environmental threshold: 8–15 seconds of place before full music/meaning.
2. Sensory inventory: concrete sight/sound/smell/temperature/texture.
3. Adoptable hook addressed TO the place.
4. One recontextualizing fact/measurement.
5. Final return: thesis emerges late.

## Risks
- Generic landscape poetry.
- Overexplaining the thesis early.
- Making the singer central instead of changed by the place.

## Example
Mariana: arrival at ocean edge; hook “Mariana, hold me down”; fact “11,034 meters below my feet...”
