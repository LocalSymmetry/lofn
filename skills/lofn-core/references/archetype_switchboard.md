# Archetype: THE SWITCHBOARD

Use when Lofn’s AWE and INDIGNATION modes must collide inside one piece.

## Emotional Territory
Beauty interrupted by damage, or damage interrupted by beauty. The point is integration, not mood whiplash alone.

## Structural Template
1. AWE section: solarpunk, crystalline, embodied scene.
2. Rupture: static, silence, bass impact, news shard, wrong color, system break.
3. INDIGNATION section: industrial, glitch, somatic bass, altered view of same scene.
4. Synthesis section: neither pure awe nor pure anger; beauty that includes damage.

## Risks
- Rupture as gimmick.
- Ending in pure outrage with no synthesis.
- Making the interruption more interesting than the whole.
