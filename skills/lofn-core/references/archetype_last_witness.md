# Archetype: THE LAST WITNESS

Use when the emotional center is a voice/person/memory that may soon disappear.

## Emotional Territory
Urgency, tenderness, preservation. Not abstract grief: the moral pressure of listening before the witness is gone.

## Structural Template
1. Open with one voice or trace, nearly unaccompanied.
2. Specific sensory memory from the witness.
3. Present world contrast: what has replaced or forgotten it.
4. Hook as prayer/invocation to the witness: “Tell me again.”
5. Bridge: witness voice fragments; music strips away.
6. Final chorus: listener continues after witness vanishes.

## Risks
- Exploitative trauma framing.
- News-report summary instead of embodied testimony.
- Making the singer’s virtue the subject.
