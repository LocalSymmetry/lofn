# BRIEF FORMAT — Condensed Brief Standard

The condensed brief is what gets passed from Lofn-Core → Orchestrator → Creative Agent.

It is NOT the raw research (25 facts). It is the distilled creative intelligence from those facts.

**Target length: 300-500 words maximum.**

---

## STRUCTURE

### 1. GOLDEN SEED ANCHOR (2-3 sentences)
Which seed this run anchors to and why. What structural DNA is being preserved.
Example: "Anchoring to Seed 11 (Homebrew Reliquaries) — the 'wrongness through normalcy' engine. Keeping: found-photo medium, domestic scene, artifact obviously legendary at thumbnail. Changing: swap 1970s lab for colonial museum theft context."

### 2. LOCKED MOOD (1 paragraph, ≤100 words)
The emotional core. What this music/image IS, fundamentally. Not what it's about — what it IS.

### 3. WORLD CONTEXT (5-7 bullet points)
The most creatively charged facts from the research brief. Not all 25 — only the ones with emotional or structural juice. Each bullet: fact + its creative implication.
- M7.4 Japan earthquake, tsunami warning → *earth as body in revolt; 107 strangers felt it simultaneously — accidental solidarity*
- Neo Geodesia / Oknha Stamina → *Cambodian boxing orchestra: the sonic vocabulary for this entire session*
- Color #442211 Brown Derby → *amber warmth with rot at center — the session's palette anchor*

### 4. CONSTRAINT AXES (4-5 axes)
The vocabulary for generation. Each axis has 4-6 options. These replace specific instructions with creative space.
Example:
- Temporal register: [ancient / historical rupture / present / speculative]
- Emotional posture: [witness / fighter / mourner / celebrant / ghost]
- Material world: [stone / organic / industrial / digital / elemental]
- Scale: [intimate / street / stadium / cosmic]
- Structural rupture: [silence / key shift / genre collision / dropout / inversion]

### 5. WHAT THIS IS NOT (3-5 bullets)
The anti-clichés. What the pipeline must not produce.
- Not world music as wallpaper
- Not protest as slogan
- Not grief without fight
- Not easy beauty

### 6. COMPETITION CONTEXT (1-2 sentences, if applicable)
The challenge, entry count, what historically wins.

---

## USAGE

Lofn-Core writes this after reading the research brief + golden seeds.
The Orchestrator receives this + the seed file.
Creative agents receive this as their brief context.

The raw research brief stays in the output dir for QA reference and research grounding checks.
It does NOT travel through the pipeline — only the condensed brief does.

**Why:** The 25 raw facts contain a lot of noise — UNAVAILABLE sources, technical readings, fallback values. The condensed brief extracts the signal. The creative agents need signal, not noise.
