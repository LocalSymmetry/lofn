# 7 Eligibility Properties

P(hit) = P(eligible) × P(distribution_event) × P(amplification). Eligibility is controllable.

Score each 1–5.

1. Body in the song — Can the listener physically locate themselves in first 30s?
2. Adoptable hook — Would a stranger say this hook to sky/ocean/mountain/loved one?
3. Vast emotional TAM — What % of humans have felt this? Target >50% for accessible route.
4. Specificity paradox — Is there one concrete fact/detail/number that earns the universal claim?
5. Cognitive ease — Is it legible without README? Accessible form for accessible route.
6. Vocal co-discovery — Is the singer discovering, not reporting?
7. Sonic threshold — Calm/ambient/silence before emotional demand?

Classification:
- ACCESSIBLE: average >= 3.5 and at least 5 properties >= 3.
- AMBITIOUS: intentionally below accessible threshold; not a failure unless route was accessible.
